<h3><strong>My unofficial <em>Vital Study Zine </em>#<em>18 </em>with observations from Vital Psychedelic Training and recent happenings in the space</strong></h3>
</div>
<p>       <figure style="width: 1280px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://www.eriskayconnection.com/home/130-transcendent-country-of-the-mind.html"><img decoding="async" src="https://newpsychonaut.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Dimension-21-from-the-series-Transcendent-Country-of-the-Mind-Enhanced.avif?format=original" alt="    From Transcendent Country of the Mind by Sari Soninen published by      The Eriskay Collection    "></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">From Transcendent Country of the Mind by Sari Soninen published by      The Eriskay Collection</figcaption></figure> </p>
<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>
<h1>‘Integration is something that the psychedelic space talks about a lot, without ever going into detail about what it is.’</h1>
<p>Thjat’s according to Jungian psychoanalyst Scott J Hill in his seminal work, <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Scott-J.-Hill/e/B0166RCTKE%3Fref=dbs_a_mng_rwt_scns_share"><em>Confrontation with the Unconscious: Jungian Depth Psychology and Psychedelic Experience</em></a>. (On which topic, much more later down the line).</p>
<p>And it’s fair to say that early integration programs were pretty toss: some progressive psychotherapy here, some wellbeing basics there, plus some toned-down underground and/or Groffian tactics drip-fed to the receptive. </p>
<p>To be fair, rectifying the psychedelic experience and life in post-industrial society is hard enough as it is. Helping a vulnerable individual to navigate it while reamaining within the bounds of a socially acceptable therapeutic practice arguably more so. </p>
<p>Nonethless, unprepared clinical trial psychologiosts and wellbeing hustlers in plausible knitwear alike mostly ended up fruitlessly recommending meditation apps to mind-blown metropoles, after they’d <a href="https://triptherapie.nl/en/top-8-documentaires-psychedelische-therapie-netflix/">seen ayahuasca on</a> <a href="https://triptherapie.nl/en/top-8-documentaires-psychedelische-therapie-netflix/">Netflix</a> and naively presumed it’d be like that <a href="https://immersive-dali.com/london/">immersive Salvador Dali show</a>. </p>
<h2>“No dramatic life changes within the first fortnight”</h2>
<p>Psychedelic integration therapy was showing the whole space up, frankly. </p>
<p>So fair play to <a href="https://www.vitalpsychedelictraining.com">Vital Psychedelic Training</a> headmaster and consciousness thought leader Kyle Buller. He took time to assemble a worthwhile integration manual while interning at MAPS, and presented on the topic to begin Module Five of Vital’s year-long psychedelic training programme. </p>
<p>Even for total edgelords, actual integration is not to be sniffed at. Because it concerns the subject of… <em>making your trip go on forever</em>. </p>
<p>Sort of. The golden you who blossoms under the medicine can be ‘integrated’ to a certain extent. </p>
<p>Using techniques like Jungian depth analysis, those seemingly fantastical visions can be understood as unconscious guidance; in a similar manner to dreams. This is good news for those of us who receive a sensation along the lines of ‘Why are you worrying about that? Think about <em>this,</em>’ when we run our carefully prepared list of neuroses – ‘intentions’ –<em> </em>past<em> </em>the medicine.</p>
<p>Likewise, going for a walk in the woods while <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?list=PLWt_gYfbC9Vbbt8AsJtlgsjV0H9Vmhrbk&amp;v=YhRybYyrNaI">listening to John Hopkins</a> <em>does</em> help one not feel so shitty to be back in reality. Troopers like Dr Sam Gandy (coming up later in the integration module) have got stats on the benefits of <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?index=1&amp;list=PLHkFG2EjLJZHhwcqtuPebX71_9w2qFaju&amp;v=GpuKUOQrfEI">setting trippers free into nature</a>. Which is the one thing us recreational yobs could’ve told you about integration. (Back the outlaw days we made do with a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?index=7&amp;list=PLHkFG2EjLJZFaVh_eIhfocTE6o5Wm-7jU&amp;v=bWebqCRw7o4">KLF CD</a>, <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?index=2&amp;list=PL735D3719258C6DA1&amp;v=mGygaPaneIo">David Lynch’s version of Dune</a> and lashings of <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EtVZbQkvk_Q">soap bar</a>.)</p>
<p>Guess what also helps with your psychedleic integration? Meditation, which, long story short, aids effective neuroplasticity, clarity and homeostasis.</p>
<p>It’s all in this laudable round-up paper from Geoff J. Bathje  out of Chicago’s Adler University, <a href="https://www.frontiersin.org/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2022.824077/full"><em>Psychedelic integration: An analysis of the concept and its practice</em></a>.</p>
<p>And here’s my edit of Top Integration Tips from the week’s barrage of alternative self-improvement intel. And remember everyone – “No dramatic life changes within the first fortnight following the experience,” says Kyle, “…preferably longer.”</p>
