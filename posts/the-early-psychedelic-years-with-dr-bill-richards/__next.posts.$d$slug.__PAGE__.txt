<h2>Dr Bill Richards is a staple of the modern-day mystery school researching psychedelics. He’s worked alongside Abraham Maslow, Stanislav Grof, Walter Pahnke and more. Now installed at the Johns Hopkins Centre for Psychedelic and Consciousness Research, he passed trade secrets on to Vital students.</h2>
<p>Week four lecturer <a href="https://hopkinspsychedelic.org/richards">Dr Bill Richards</a> volunteered for LSD testing as a restless theology student in post-WW2 Germany. He began working alongside Hanscarl Leuner, the German psychologist who invented Guided Affective Imagery (a perverse form of which was used in the brainwashing sequence of <em>A Clockwork Orange</em>), plus added both art and group therapy to LSD tests. </p>
<p>Richards went on to become the most prolific psychedelic researcher of all time, working alongside Walter Pahnke, Abraham Maslow, Stanislav Grof, and now Roland Griffiths: he was last out of Spring Grove in 1977, first into the fledgling <a href="https://hopkinspsychedelic.org/">Johns Hopkins Centre for Psychedelic and Consciousness Research</a> in 1999, and is still working today. His book <a href="https://newpsychonaut.com/blog/Sacred%20Knowledge:%20Psychedelics%20and%20Religious%20Experiences"><em>Sacred Knowledge: Psychedelics and Religious Experiences</em></a> is out now.</p>
<p>See Bill interviewed <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?index=14&amp;list=PLHkFG2EjLJZFuv59kEov9TWSnsVc2R2qc&amp;v=q9uexi_VHkg">here</a> plus a bunch more I put on <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLHkFG2EjLJZGlNz3GB6PhECdhN5f3leJu">this YouTube resource channel.</a></p>
