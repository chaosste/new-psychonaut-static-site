<h2>“Microdosing is a step forward for humankind.”</h2>
<p>Beckley Psytech’s Lady Amanda Fielding (for it is she)  declared so at Psych Symposium. If psychedelics can be for the everyman, can they be for every day? </p>
<p>While it’s less spectacular than ‘spiritual doses’, DMT, or ayahuasca, and an ongoing science to say the least, microdosing’s arguably taken a stronger foothold in popular culture than the next-level psychedelics. Users report similar effects to integrated major experiences, like enthusiasm, geniality, consideration and walking in the woods while listening to Jon Hopkins. Famously though microdosing – which Beckley are <a href="https://www.beckleyfoundation.org/microdosing/">researching</a> throughly – is one of the few contemporary psychedelic phenomena to fail the placebo test. Small doses are being tested on some conditions: MindMed are on <a href="https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mindmed-initiates-phase-2a-lsd-trial-for-the-treatment-of-adult-adhd-301447252.html">stage two</a> for 20µg of LSD twice a week, while sticking someone in a room and giving them a proper tab (200µg) <a href="https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/mindmed-collaborator-dr-matthias-liechti-to-present-topline-clinical-trial-results-for-lsd-in-anxiety-disorders-at-psych-symposium-301525471.html">did okay for GAD</a> . The likes of <a href="https://www.thenewhealthclub.de/">New Health Club</a> are poised to bring acid to the workplace (at last). Lenny Gibson’s observation was that ‘psyche’ also means ‘breath’, and <a href="https://www.stangrof.com/">Stanislav Grof’s</a> holotropic breathwork could be the only option – and also a better one – for many.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.drugscience.org.uk/psychedelics-and-the-brain-david-nutt/">Professor David Nutt</a> pointed out in his Psych presentation that it’s the ‘wellbeing’ scores that are really impressive in psychedelic therapy’s efficacy results. But according to a neuroscientist I spoke to outside when the fire alarm went off, “there are no criteria for developing drugs for ‘wellbeing’ like there might be for mental health conditions already treated pharmacologically. So everybody’s trying to make drugs, which is ruinously expensive as it is, without knowing precisely what to aim at, certainly in terms of approval.” The Mushroom Nation is at once already here, and still so far away. </p>
<p>NB Psychedelics are prohibited in most places even if labelled ‘cacao’ and sent in gaudy packaging. You can still get busted for them like <a href="https://psychedelicstoday.com/2022/05/17/pt319/">this guy</a>.</p>
