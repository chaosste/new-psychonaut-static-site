<h4>The next stage of human consciousness is calling. Are we brave enough to answer? </h4>
<p>Dr David Luke is the most intrepid researcher of the psychedelic renaissance – ‘the real new psychonaut’. Straight out of London but living “on the edge… of Sussex” his inspirational investigations include DMT space exploration, the psychedelic divine feminine, biophilia (tree hugging) and psionic powers – often conducted “in the field”.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.gre.ac.uk/people/rep/faculty-of-education-and-health/dr-david-luke">David Luke</a> dropped out of lecturing to study shamanism, and returned to Britain with consciousness expanded. Since he’s been at the vanguard of the psychedelic renaissance, consistently leading by example. </p>
</div>
<p>      <a href="https://www.gre.ac.uk/people/rep/faculty-of-education-and-health/dr-david-luke"><img decoding="async" src="https://newpsychonaut.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/Dr-David-Luke.avif?format=original" alt=""></a></p>
<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>

And he spoke to Vital students about it.</p>
