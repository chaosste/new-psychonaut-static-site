<h4><strong>My unofficial <em>Vital Study Zine </em>#<em>17 </em>with observations from Vital Psychedelic Training and recent happenings in the space</strong></h4>
</div>
<p>       <figure style="width: 967px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img decoding="async" src="https://newpsychonaut.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/meow-portrait-wolf-denver-Enhanced.avif?format=original" alt="    From Highlights of the Convergence, a new immersive exhibition for the visually impaired, open now at      Meow Wolf     , Denver, Colorado   "><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">From Highlights of the Convergence, a new immersive exhibition for the visually impaired, open now at      Meow Wolf     , Denver, Colorado</figcaption></figure> </p>
<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>
<h2>“I want to be a steward for reality,” declares clinical psychologist Dr Adele La France. </h2>
<p>The effervescent <a href="https://psychedelicstoday.com/2022/03/18/pt302/">Dr La France</a> is famously ‘based’. </p>
<p>She cracks off her talk to Vital students about the practicalities – the reality if you will – of psychedelic therapy by explaining how she was generally anti-psychedelic drugs, until she experienced them in a medical context. </p>
<p>Her clinician’s manner is a masterclass in marrying authority, whimsy and vision. She’s the co-creator of <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Emotion-Focused-Therapy-Katherine-Henderson-Lafrance/dp/143383085X/ref=sr_1_2?qid=1667229223&amp;refinements=p_27:Adele+Lafrance&amp;s=books&amp;sr=1-2&amp;text=Adele+Lafrance">Emotion Focussed Family Therapy</a>; her clinical manual on it is published by the notoriously hard-to-please American Psychological Association. Dr Lafrance’s new book <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/s?i=stripbooks&amp;rh=p_27:Adele+Lafrance&amp;s=relevancerank&amp;text=Adele+Lafrance&amp;ref=dp_byline_sr_book_1"><em>What to Say to Kids When Nothing Seems to Work: A Practical Guide for Parents and Caregivers</em></a> is out now at a family-friendly price.</p>
<p>While toiling at the sharp end of mental health, she’s spoken convincingly (and warmly) about topical issues like <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5X8IGqkz0tU&amp;list=PLHkFG2EjLJZFRUB6-u4k9jeGHjPahOzZR&amp;index=4">taking ayahuasca to heal eating disorders on Emmy Award-winning daytime TV show <em>The Doctors</em></a>, and video game addiction on CBS. She’s currently working as clinical investigator and strategy lead at MAPS’ study of MDMA-assisted psychotherapy for eating disorders, and as clinical support on Imperial College London’s study for psilocybin and <em>anorexia nervosa</em>.</p>
<p>Maybe because Dr Lafrance has actually been conducting psychedelic therapy while the rest of us are debating its finer points, the softly-outspoken clinician has junked some of the practice’s outdated rhetoric. </p>
<h2>“Oftentimes they’ll be rewarded with amazing insights in the bathroom” </h2>
<p>Like its reluctance to let voyagers take a pee, lest a break for ‘voiding’ stops them from ‘surrendering to the medicine’. “If they’re doing work that feels really meaningful, clients may get conflicted about going to the bathroom. But my stance is that the meeting of physiological needs is the most important,” she says, “After all, Abram Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs would say insights are great, but engaging consistently in meeting physical needs is fundamental.”</p>
<p>According to Dr Lafrance there are all sorts of other advantages to be discovered when trippers stumble to the lavatory.</p>
<p>“Oftentimes they’ll be rewarded with amazing insights in the bathroom,” she says, and it’s true ablutions are managed by the right brain, hence Pythagoras’ eureka-in-the-bath moment.</p>
<p>Things clicked for Dr Lafrance – “<a href="https://dradelelafrance.com">Adele</a>, please” she urges Vital students – in a ‘patient experience’ she had a while taking part in an MDMA safety trial. </p>
</div>
<p>       <figure style="width: 883px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="https://dradelelafrance.com"><img decoding="async" src="https://newpsychonaut.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/adele-press-pic-Enhanced.avif?format=original" alt="     Dr Adele La France    "></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">Dr Adele La France</figcaption></figure> </p>
<div class="sqs-html-content" data-sqsp-text-block-content>
<p>“It happened to me when I was part of an MDMA study for health controls,” she confesses, “I went to the bathroom to pee. But I was working on something, and I couldn’t wait to finish it up and get back. So I was pee-ing in a pressured way… I can’t believe I’m sharing this… anyways… I realised, <em>for fuck’s sake’s –</em> <em>I couldn’t even pee in peace</em>. I’m so focussed on productivity and getting things done, that I can’t even pee at a rate that is organic! That revelation was life-changing. Now I never want to multi-task ever again. It’s self injury!”</p>
<p>It is indeed. But there’s even more to a mindful tinkle than flushing out the pipes, psychic or otherwise. </p>
<p>“If we all heal the split between our mind and our body, not only are we more attuned to when we need to void or eat, we’re more attuned to our instincts,” advises the working psychedelic clinician, “and reconnecting with their instincts the greatest gift we can give our clients.” </p>
<p>Homeostasis for everyone. And, risk of multi-tasking aside, Adele has further intentions: she’s grown psychedelic therapy into a fresh model, ‘theoretically informed’ psychedelic therapy, incorporating her angle of emotion processing. </p>
<h2>“A sign of emotional maturity is the capacity to hold anger and love at the same time”</h2>
<p>She’ll apparently be presenting this next year in 2023, and gave the Vital cohort a sneak peek.</p>
<p>“I am committed to reality,” she reminds us, “meaning, actively letting go of blame narratives and fantasy as a tool for human relationships. A sign of emotional maturity is the capacity to hold anger and love at the same time, reckoning with the complexity of human relationships.”</p>
<p>Watch <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r8HZRLTLHD8&amp;list=PLHkFG2EjLJZFRUB6-u4k9jeGHjPahOzZR&amp;index=6">Adele talking to Gabor Maté on behalf of Chacruna</a> and more including her talks for MAPS on my <a href="https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLHkFG2EjLJZFRUB6-u4k9jeGHjPahOzZR">New Psychonaut YouTube lecture library type thing</a>.</p>
