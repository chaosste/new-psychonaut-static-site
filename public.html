EntheoGen

Plant Medicine Interaction Guide, web app, and abstention-aware dataset.

This is a standalone public-facing EntheoGen page designed for students, university-affiliated researchers, and thoughtful collaborators who want a clean overview without the wider New Psychonaut site header or footer.

EntheoGen is a harm-reduction-oriented interaction guide focused on intentional psychedelic use. It combines a live web app, a rule-based evidence posture, and an emerging dataset designed for benchmarking, mechanistic reasoning, and safe abstention behavior.

Safety posture

EntheoGen provides educational harm-reduction guidance only. It does not replace medical, psychiatric, or therapeutic advice. When evidence is limited, the system is intended to abstain rather than generate false certainty. In any urgent clinical situation, seek professional medical help.
