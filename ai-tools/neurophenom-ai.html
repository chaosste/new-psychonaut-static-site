High-fidelity clinical interface for neurophenomenology.

Map pre-reflective subjective experience through granular interview techniques. Use the trained AI research interviewer or record and transcribe. AI analyses and codifies each interview.

NeuroPhenom AI is a high-fidelity clinical interface for mapping pre-reflective subjective experience, with altered states of consciousness as a core use case. It combines trained microphenomenology interviewing with granular LLM analysis, diachronic slicing, and structural synthesis.

Featuring a stark black-and-white minimalist design, the interface stays out of the way and lets the phenomenological work take centre stage.

Clinical posture

The trained interviewer focuses on granular microphenomenology techniques for pre-reflective experience, especially altered states.

Data sovereignty

Interviews are saved locally to support privacy-aware research workflows and researcher control.

Features

Built for granular phenomenological work

Live Interview Sessions

AI-guided microphenomenology interviews in real time using trained elicitation patterns.

Standalone Recorder

Capture and transcribe audio independently of the live interviewer workflow.

Analysis View

Codify, inspect, and theme subject material with AI assistance after each session.

Diachronic Slicing

Break experiential moments into temporal phases to study shifts in lived structure.

Structural Synthesis

Map the architecture of subjective states across fragments, themes, and recurring patterns.

Consent Protocols

Built-in consent and ethical framing for sensitive phenomenological research contexts.

Local Storage

Interview material stays local in-browser, supporting data sovereignty and researcher control.

Configurable Settings

Adjust language, voice gender, and interview mode without cluttering the core workflow.

npm install

# Configure your Gemini API key

npm run dev
