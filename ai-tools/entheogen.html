EntheoGen

Plant Medicine Interaction Guide, web app, and abstention-aware dataset.

EntheoGen is a substance interaction guidance app focussed on intentional use of psychedelics. It estimates the effects of mixing two selected substances using a deterministic, evidence-grounded ruleset.

This showcase brings together the live product, downloadable handouts, beta interface previews, and the emerging Version 2.0 dataset work now shaping benchmarking, abstention analysis, and mechanistic reasoning evaluation.

Why EntheoGen Is Different

A stricter, quieter interaction guide for psychedelic contexts

Unlike generic substance checkers or purely generative tools, EntheoGen is being shaped around rule-based clarity, explicit abstention, and transparency about evidence quality.

Covers ceremonial and intentional use

Existing sources often focus on widely used recreational substances. EntheoGen is mapped to sacramental, medicinal, and commonly encountered consciousness-altering modalities.

Evidence-grounded risk engine

Interaction output is deterministic and rule-based, built from curated pharmacological logic instead of free-form prediction.

Strict safety posture

Pairs with insufficient evidence resolve to UNK, meaning Unknown, rather than returning a misleading answer.

Source traceability

Readouts are designed to expose confidence and evidentiary source metadata so the reasoning stays inspectable.

Privacy focused

Favourites can stay in local browser storage. Personal data is not stored remotely by the app, though hosting or CDN logs may still exist.

Minimalist and accessible

The interface aims for clean contrast, distinguishable risk levels, and non-prescriptive language that supports harm reduction without drama.

Release highlights

Provenance-aware interaction resolution

Classifications now encode whether a result comes from explicit, fallback, unknown, or self-pair logic, making epistemic stance measurable.

Normalized mechanism taxonomy

Mechanisms are grouped into structured categories such as serotonergic, MAOI, glutamatergic, stimulant_stack, and cardiovascular_load.

Deterministic export and diagnostics

Version 2.0 introduces reproducible exports, validation checks, distribution summaries, and regression snapshots to detect silent drift.

HuggingFace and evaluation packaging

The dataset is being prepared for trainer pipelines, slice-based evaluation, oracle baselines, and benchmark-style model comparison workflows.

Safety posture

EntheoGen provides educational harm-reduction guidance only. It is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice.

If there is no explicit evidence for a combination, the system is designed to return Unknown or Insufficient Data rather than a speculative prediction. In moments of clinical emergency, seek urgent professional medical help.
