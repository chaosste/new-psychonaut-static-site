Before You Begin

NewPsychonaut.com is not authored by doctors or trained mental health professionals. Its editor(s) possess journalist-level knowledge of psychedelics and psychotherapy.

It should not be taken as medical or psychological expertise.

If you need help or professional support with psychedelics, start with the organisations listed below. They do not provide psychedelics. Neither do I.
