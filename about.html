The Blog

New Psychonaut is a blog covering my ongoing psychedelic education.

My name is Steve Langsford Beale. You can read more about my background and intentions in this blog post.

I'm supposed to be blogging about the MSc too. It became clear early on that assignments and work experience took priority, but I'll try to report in on the university's Transdisciplinary Psychedelic Colloquium and the Philosophy of Psychedelics BA course as well.

Current Focus

My student research focuses on men using psychedelics as an alternative to existing services.

I've presented What Can Services Learn from Men Seeking Psychedelic Treatments? at Exeter University and How Psychedelics Help Men Engage with Spirituality at Boston College's London campus.

Psychedelic Studies

I'm a journalist studying MSc Psychedelics: mind, medicine and culture at the University of Exeter.

I'm currently writing my dissertation on consciousness research methods under tutor Dr. Peter Sjöstedt-Hughes.

Psychology Background

In winter 2024 I graduated from the MSc. Psychology course at the University of Exeter, specialising in cognitive experimental psychology.

I'm an accredited member of the British Psychological Society.

Vital Training

In summer 2023 I graduated from the first Vital Psychedelic Training program.

The Unofficial Vital Student Zine doubled as my coursework, including material shaped by private lectures with researchers Rick Strassman and David Luke, and clinicians Adele La France and Ben Sessa.

Disclaimers

Importantly I am not a mental health professional. Anything you read on this site, especially written by me, should not be taken as official mental health advice.

Refer yourself to an authorised doctor, therapist, or counsellor for feedback on your own health. I have put a bunch of links on the Resources page.

Moreover I cannot facilitate psychedelic experiences, and obviously not get you any drugs. I urge you to travel to a country where psychedelics are not prohibited. Would you believe these guys are on the case in the UK, kudos to them.

Most art on the site is available to buy or see on display now. Click on the links to go through to the artist or gallery website. I do not get a kickback or anything.

Harm Reduction

Psychedelics are still illegal in the UK and most countries. This website is for informational and harm-reduction purposes only.

I do support the reclassification of psychedelics based on science and evidence. But I cannot and do not encourage the use of illegal substances or breaking the law.

Neither am I evangelising for psychedelics, which are not a panacea, or recommending them to you. If you are considering psychedelic treatments via the legal medical system or in a different country, refer to the Resources page for expert advice, but the best person to speak to is probably your own therapist or doctor.
